# User Survey Form for Moving & Transportation Services

## Overview

We are SwiftMove - a house moving and delivery services platform where clients can easily schedule their house moving trips. SwiftMove is targeted towards people who are looking for an affordable moving service, and we are committed to providing a fair and transparent platform for both clients and drivers. Here are some of the key features that will help us achieve this goal:

- You can decide the maximum budget you can afford for your move, and we will make sure to only show you offers with a lower price than your budget.
- You can specify your luggage details and time in advance so you can be sure that the driver can accommodate your needs and won't cancel at the last minute.

This survey is designed to help us better understand clients' preferences, expectations, and challenges in the moving and transportation services industry. Your feedback will be invaluable in shaping our platform to better serve clients like you.
