# Driver Survey Form for Moving & Transportation Services

## Overview

We are SwiftMove - a house moving and delivery services platform where drivers can find transportation opportunities. SwiftMove will provide you house moving opportunities as well as stable delivery contracts for regional shops.

SwiftMove's primary goal is to be fair and transparent for both clients and drivers. Here are some of the key features that will help us achieve this goal:

- You decide your own price in a simple price/km rate, which you can change anytime based on the market conditions, your preferences, and your affordability.
- You can see the luggage details before you accept a trip offer, so you can always make an informed decision on whether to accept or reject a trip offer.
- You have an equal say in scheduling and coordinating the trips. Rather than having a on/off switch to get trips instantly, you can schedule trips for days in advance.
- Providing loading/unloading services other than transportation is optional.

This survey is designed to help us better understand drivers' preferences, expectations, and challenges in the moving and transportation services industry. Your feedback will be invaluable in shaping our platform to better serve drivers like you.
