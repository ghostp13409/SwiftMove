## Part 3: The Solution

**Slide 7: A Fair Marketplace**

- **Headline:** The SwiftMove Solution
- **Points:**
  - **Open Marketplace:** Drivers set their rates; Clients set their budgets.
  - **Blind Negotiation:** Prevents price gouging; ensures true market value.
  - **Data-Driven Logic:** Validation of volume and furniture _before_ the offer.
  - **Sustainability:** Zero-overhead architecture focused on service, not profit.
