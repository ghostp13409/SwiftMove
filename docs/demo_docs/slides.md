## Problem Slides

**Slide 4**:
Title - Students Move Houses

**Slide 5**:
Header: The Problem
Content:
**Predatory Fees**
Drivers only receive 60% of the total trip cost on mainstream platforms like Uber and Lyft. That means You pay 40% more.
**Lack of Control**
No one get to choose prices. Not you, nor the driver.
**No Transparency**
No way to verify vehicle capacity or furniture friendliness for your important housing trips.

---

## Solution Slides

**Slide 6**:
Title – Solution?
Sub Text: A Fair Marketplace

**Slide 7**:
Header: SwiftMove
Content:

**Open Marketplace**
Unlike competitors, We don't set prices. Drivers set their price per km; Clients set their max budget.
**Blind Negotiation**
Clients can't see driver rates, and drivers can't see client budgets. That means no chance of price gouging.
**Clear Communication**
The Clients know which car they are getting, Drivers know what luggage they are carrying.
**Sustainability, Not Profit**
Designed like a zombie. A low-maintenance platform, so you don’t have to pay more.
