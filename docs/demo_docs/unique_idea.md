# Unique Idea

## Transparent and Fair Open Marketplace

The unique idea what seperates SwiftMove from other services is that we provide a transparent and fair open marketplace for clients and drivers. So the main key difference is we provide an open market instead of setting prices ourselves. This allows price consious clients like students and the freelancer drivers to have more control over the prices and make it more affordable for them. This also allows us to have a wider range of clients and drivers on our platform which would increase the chances of matching clients with drivers and increase the overall satisfaction of both parties.

## Fair by Design

We have designed our platform to be fair for both clients and drivers. The driver can set their own prices per km for their vehicles based on what they can afford. The clients can set their own maximum budget that they can afford for their move. This allows both parties to have control over the prices and make it more affordable for them. The Move Requests created by clients would only be visible to the drivers that are within the max budget of the clients. This allows us to ensure that the clients are not being overcharged and the drivers are not being underpaid. The client can't see drivers price per km and the driver can't see the client's max budget. This allows us to ensure that both parties are not being influenced by each other's prices and make it more fair for both parties. The clients are encourged to set a reasonable max budget so that they can get more offeres more easily and the drivers are encourged to set a reasonable price per km so that they can get more move requests more easily.

We also have a rating system in place that allows clients and drivers to rate each other after each move. This allows us to maintain a high level of quality on our platform and ensure that both parties are satisfied with the service they receive.

## Not in the Business to Make Money

We are not in business to make money, we are in business to provide a service that is affordable and fair for both clients and drivers. We believe that by providing a transparent and fair open marketplace, we can create a win-win situation for both parties and create a sustainable business model that benefits everyone. We are not looking to maximize profits at the expense of our clients and drivers, we are looking to create a platform that is beneficial for everyone involved.

For this reason, we have decided to keep the commissions to a minimum just to keep the servers running and cover the costs of maintaining the platform. We are not looking to make a profit from our clients and drivers. We are simply here to provide a solution. We have also decided to make our platform open source so that anyone can contribute to it and make it better. This also allows the community to not just rely our decisions and gives better control to the users of our platform. We believe that by being open source, we can create a more transparent and fair platform for everyone involved. Last but not least, making it open source also allows the community to implement and scale the platform in different regions, so the platform is not limited by our resources and ability to scale and can grow organically based on the needs of the users.
